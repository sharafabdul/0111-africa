Our Mission

Bridging the digital divide for Africa's hidden talent

0111 Africa is a forward-thinking technology and impact organization building digital tools and platforms that bridge opportunity gaps for underserved communities across Africa—with a focus on rural and low-connectivity populations.

What We Do

AI-Powered Talent Discovery

Machine learning algorithms that identify potential across sports, technology, creativity, and vocational skills — removing human bias from the equation.

Offline-First Digital Platforms

Applications designed for low or no connectivity environments, ensuring rural communities aren't left behind by the digital revolution.

Youth, Sports & Skills Development

Comprehensive programs that nurture talent from discovery through development, creating pathways to opportunity.
